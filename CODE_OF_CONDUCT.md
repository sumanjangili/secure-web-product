# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone—regardless of background, identity, or experience level. By participating in this project, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** – Treat others with courtesy and empathy.
- **Communicate constructively** – Give and receive feedback in a professional manner.
- **Stay on topic** – Keep discussions focused on the project's goals and technical aspects.
- **Protect privacy** – Do not share personal data or confidential information without consent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Disparaging remarks about race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic.
- Sharing malicious code, phishing attempts, or instructions that facilitate wrongdoing.
- Posting personal or private information about others without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers and community members are empowered to:

- Issue warnings.
- Remove offending content.
- Suspend or block contributors from the repository or related communication channels.

Decisions will be made transparently and with an eye toward rehabilitation whenever possible.

## Reporting Incidents

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code:

1. **Contact a maintainer** directly (e.g., via GitHub @username or the project’s mailing list).
2. Provide a clear description of the incident, including timestamps, usernames, and any relevant context.
3. Maintainers will investigate promptly and take appropriate action.

All reports will be handled confidentially and with respect for privacy.
